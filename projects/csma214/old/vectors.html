VECTORS

You may have heard of vectors before, maybe in math, or even games. Vectors are very important in both contexts. A vector is an object that has both direction and magnitude. There's also two points to a vector: the tail and the head.



The direction of the vector indicates which way it's facing.
The magnitude of the vector indicates the size of the vector (how long it is)
Pretty simple, right?

You can also add vectors, by drawing a line from the first vector's head to the second vector's tail.



Subtracting vectors is also possible by reversing the vector we want to subtract, and then adding the two vectors as usual.



Multiplying and dividing vectors, however, are a little more complicated.
