h's car show CSMA 212 (Media Environments) | Professor Ivaylo Getov | Spring 2021
proposal:

This is a concept of an underground car exhibition made in TouchDesigner. I tried to use projection mapping as creatively as I could, by projecting on the cars and the walls. I also played around with ambient lighting and colors to further enforce an "underground" feeling. Even creativity has it's limits in TouchDesigner, I was going to place more lights in my scene but I was limited by TouchDesigner's 13 light limit.
